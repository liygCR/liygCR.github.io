About Me

I am currently a Post-Doc Fellow at the Department of Statistics and Finance, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), and before that I was a Post-Doc Fellow at Dalla Lana School of Public Health, University of Toronto. Previously, I was a Ph.D student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Yaohua Wu and Prof. Baisuo Jin.

My research interests revolve around a variety of topics in High-dimensional statistical learning, Change-point, Functional/spatial data analysis, Precision (personalized) medicine.
